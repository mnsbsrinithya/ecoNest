Why Track Your Usage?

By tracking your electricity consumption, you can identify patterns and find opportunities to save on your energy bills, reduce waste, and minimize your environmental impact.

What Are the Key Appliances?

Air conditioners, lights, fridges, and fans all consume electricity in varying amounts. By monitoring their usage, you can make informed decisions to cut back on unnecessary consumption.

How Can I Save Energy?

Simple steps like turning off appliances when not in use, using energy-efficient devices, or switching to LED lights can dramatically reduce energy consumption.
